Methodology

Clinical Excellence,
Wrapped in Play.

We don't just teach skills; we nurture potential. Our data-driven, assent-based model ensures therapy is effective, engaging, and respectful.

The Clinical Spectrum

Just as a prism refracts light into color, our model breaks development down into a full spectrum of growth. We meet your child exactly where they are.

Communication

From functional requests ("I want juice") to complex conversation. We give every child a voice, whether vocal or using AAC.

Social Skills

Navigating the playground, reading social cues, and building meaningful friendships in a natural, supported setting.

Behavior Regulation

Replacing challenging behaviors with functional alternatives. We focus on identifying the "why" behind the behavior.

Independence

Self-help skills like toileting, feeding, and dressing. Building confidence to navigate the world autonomously.

Play Skills

Expanding interests from rigid routines to imaginative, collaborative play that opens doors to peer connection.

School Readiness

Preparing for the classroom environment: circle time attention, following group instructions, and transitions.

Our Philosophy

Assent-Based & Compassionate

At Early Start, we practice Assent-Based ABA. This means we prioritize your child's willingness to participate above all else.

If a child withdraws, cries, or shows distress, we stop. We reassess. We change our approach. We do not use "escape extinction" or force compliance through tears.

By building a foundation of safety and trust (pairing), we find that children are eager to learn. Therapy becomes something they run toward, not away from.

No Forced Compliance
Respect for Bodily Autonomy
Joy is the Metric of Success

"Happy, Relaxed, and Engaged"

This is the only state in which true learning happens. It is our job to get there, every single session.

We Measure What Matters

While we focus on joy, we are grounded in science. Our clinical team uses advanced digital data collection to track progress in real-time, ensuring transparency and accountability.

Daily Data Entry
Weekly Supervision

Real-Time Graphs

We don't guess if a skill is mastered. We graph it. You can see visual proof of your child's growth over time.

Transparent Reporting

No clinical jargon. We provide clear, plain-language reports on goals, barriers, and achievements during monthly parent meetings.

Dynamic Adjustments

If a child isn't progressing, we don't wait. We analyze the data and adjust the teaching strategy immediately.

You Are The Expert On Your Child

We might be the experts in ABA, but you are the expert on your child. Our approach is collaborative. We provide robust Parent Coaching to ensure that the skills learned in the clinic generalize to your home and community life.
