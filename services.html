What We Do

Holistic Therapy,
Integrated Care.

We offer a full spectrum of pediatric services under one roof. Whether you need focused behavioral support or a comprehensive school-readiness plan, we have a pathway for you.

ABA Therapy

Evidence-based Applied Behavior Analysis focused on skill acquisition, behavior reduction, and joy. Our assent-based model prioritizes your child's dignity.

  • 1:1 Individualized Support
  • Behavior Regulation
  • Parent Training Included
Explore ABA Therapy

Speech Therapy

Giving every child a voice. We treat articulation disorders, language delays, and social communication challenges in a fun, engaging environment.

  • Articulation & Phonology
  • Expressive/Receptive Language
  • AAC Device Training
Explore Speech

Occupational Therapy

Developing the "jobs" of childhood: play and independence. We focus on fine motor skills, sensory processing, and daily living tasks.

  • Sensory Integration
  • Fine Motor Coordination
  • Self-Care Independence
Explore OT

The Bridge Program

A specialized school-readiness track for children transitioning to general education. We simulate classroom environments to teach group skills.

  • Mock Classroom Routines
  • Peer Play & Group Instruction
  • Transition Planning
View Program Details

Flexible Service Settings

We offer therapy in three distinct environments to best suit your family's needs and your child's goals.

Clinic Based

Structured environments with sensory gyms and mock classrooms designed for focused skill acquisition.

Home Based

Therapy in your natural environment. Perfect for working on daily routines, sleep, and family dynamics.

School Integrated

Push-in support at Chroma Academy. We generalize skills to the classroom in real-time.

Unsure where to start?

Our clinical team offers free 15-minute consultations to help you understand which service is right for your child.
