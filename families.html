The "Magic Window"

Why Early Intervention Matters

The first few years of a child's life are a period of rapid brain development known as neuroplasticity. During this time, the brain is incredibly adaptable, making it the ideal window for learning new skills and overcoming developmental hurdles.

Research consistently shows that children who receive intensive, high-quality intervention before age 5 have significantly better long-term outcomes in communication, social skills, and independence.

At Chroma Early Start, we leverage this critical time to build a strong foundation. We don't just focus on "catching up"; we focus on giving your child the tools to thrive for a lifetime.

The Benefits of Starting Early

  • Improved Communication Reducing frustration by giving children a way to express their wants and needs.
  • Social Connection Building the skills to play with peers, share experiences, and make friends.
  • School Readiness Developing the "learning to learn" behaviors needed for a classroom environment.
  • Happier Homes Reducing maladaptive behaviors creates a more peaceful, connected family life.

Your Intake Journey

From first call to first day, we make the process seamless.

Step 1

Consultation

A free 15-minute call to discuss your concerns and see if we are a good fit. We'll answer all your initial questions about logistics, approach, and availability.

Timeline: Day 1
Step 2

Verification & Auth

We check your insurance benefits and submit the assessment request. We handle the communication with the payer so you don't have to wait on hold.

Timeline: 3-5 Days
Step 3

Skills Assessment

Our BCBA meets your child for a play-based skills assessment to identify strengths, barriers, and goals. We use tools like the VB-MAPP or ABLLS-R to build a baseline.

Timeline: 1 Week
Step 4

Start Therapy!

We build a schedule that works for your family, pair you with a clinical team (BCBA & RBTs), and begin the journey of growth and connection.

Timeline: Immediate openings
The First Month

What to Expect: The First 30 Days

Starting therapy is a big transition. Here is exactly what happens during your first month with us.

W1

Pairing & Fun

Zero demands. Our only goal is for your child to fall in love with their therapist and the center. We play, we explore, and we build trust. This foundation is critical for future learning.

W2

Routine Building

We gently introduce the daily schedule—arrival, circle time, snack. We start identifying what motivates your child to learn and establishing patterns of reinforcement.

W4

Initial Goals

We introduce the first few learning targets. You'll receive your first data update and have a check-in with your BCBA to review early progress and adjust the plan if needed.

Family Logistics FAQ

What is your sickness policy?

To protect our immunocompromised clients, we have a strict 24-hour symptom-free policy for fever, vomiting, and contagious illnesses. Please keep your child home if they are unwell.

How does drop-off and pick-up work?

We use a secure QR code check-in system. An RBT will meet you at the reception area to transition your child into the clinic. For safety, only authorized guardians with ID can pick up a child.

Do you offer transportation?

We do not provide home transport. However, if your child attends Chroma Early Learning Academy, our staff will handle the transition between the classroom and the therapy clinic for you.

Ready to take the first step?

We know this process is new for many families. We are here to answer every question, no matter how small.
