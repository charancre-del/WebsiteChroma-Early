Curriculum

What We Practice

Group Instruction

Learning to learn in a group. Sitting for circle time, raising hands, and following choral responses.

Transitions

Moving from high-preference activities (recess) to work (table time) without maladaptive behavior.

Social Navigation

Sharing materials, asking a peer to play, and understanding personal space boundaries.

Functional Independence

Managing a backpack, opening lunch containers, toileting, and hand-washing independently.

Is Your Child Ready for Bridge?

The Bridge Program is typically for children ages 3-6 who have mastered foundational 1:1 skills and are preparing for a less restrictive environment.

Prerequisite Skills

  • Minimal aggressive/disruptive behavior
  • Can sit for 2-3 minutes with reinforcement
  • Basic functional communication (vocal or AAC)

Targeted Outcomes

  • Follows group instructions without 1:1 prompts
  • Engages in sustained peer play
  • Independently transitions between activities
