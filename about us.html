Established 2022

More than a clinic.
A second home.

We founded Chroma on a simple belief: Clinical therapy should be a perfect blend of rigorous skill development and the comforting warmth of family.

Our Purpose

"To cultivate a vibrant community of lifelong learners by blending clinical excellence with the nurturing warmth of home, ensuring every child feels seen, valued, and capable."

From One Classroom to a Community

Chroma Early Learning Academy began with a single location in Canton and a mission to redefine "daycare." We didn't just want to watch children; we wanted to ignite them. As we grew into a network across Metro Atlanta, we saw a vital need for specialized support.

Chroma Early Start was created to bring that same "second home" philosophy to the world of ABA therapy. We are locally owned, operated by educators and clinicians, and driven by the success of our families. Unlike private equity chains, every decision here is made with a face and a name in mind.

The Heart of Chroma

We don't just hire technicians; we hire career clinicians. Our team is the most valuable asset in our clinics, selected for their passion, patience, and professional credentials.

Certified & Credentialed

Every therapist holds RBT or BCBA certification. We support ongoing education for every staff member.

Safety First

Rigorous federal/state background checks. All staff are certified in CPR, First Aid, and Safety Care.

Continuous Growth

Our clinicians participate in 20+ hours of annual professional development specializing in modern ABA.

Our Non-Negotiables

These four pillars guide every decision we make.

Unconditional Joy

Radical Safety
